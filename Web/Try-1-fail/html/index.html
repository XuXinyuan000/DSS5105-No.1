ESG Rating Explanation

    ESG stands for Environmental, Social, and Governance. It is a set of criteria used to evaluate a company's performance in these three critical areas:

  • Environmental: Measures how well a company manages its impact on the environment
  • Social: Assesses a company's responsibility toward social factors
  • Governance: Focuses on a company's corporate governance structure and practices
ESG Indicators

    We have selected some indicators from ESG as rating criteria. Here are the indicators:

  • Environmental: Greenhouse Gas Emissions, Energy Consumption, Water Consumption, Waste Generation
  • Social: Gender Diversity , Age-Based Diversity, Employment, Development & Training, Occupational Health & Safety
  • Governance: Board independence, Women on the board, Ethical Behaviour, Assurance
